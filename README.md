# Telegram Email Forwarding Bot

A bot that forwards emails from your Gmail account to Telegram.

## Privacy Policy

### What data we collect?
- Email addresses (the Gmail accounts you add)
- OAuth tokens (to access your Gmail)
- Telegram user ID and username

### How we use your data?
- Forward emails from your Gmail to Telegram
- Your data is stored securely on our server
- We never share your data with third parties

### How to revoke access?
1. Go to Google Account settings
2. Click "Security" → "Third-party apps"
3. Find "Telegram Email Bot" and click "Remove"
