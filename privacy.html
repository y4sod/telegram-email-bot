📧 Privacy Policy

Telegram Email Forwarding Bot

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Accounts: Gmail addresses you add to the bot
  • OAuth Tokens: Temporary access tokens to read your emails
  • Telegram Data: Your Telegram user ID, username, and name
  • Email Content: Email subject, sender, and body (only for forwarding)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Forward your Gmail emails to your Telegram account
  • Store OAuth tokens securely to maintain access
  • Never share your data with third parties

3. Data Storage

  • All data is stored on our secure servers
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted
  • You can delete your data anytime by removing email accounts

4. Your Rights

  • Revoke access: myaccount.google.com/permissions
  • Delete email accounts from the bot at any time
  • Contact us for data deletion requests
